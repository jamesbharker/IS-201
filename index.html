About

Having grown up in Southern California with access to unique opportunities in film education and work, James Harker has spent the better part of a decade developing and honing his craft. His proficienies include, but are not limited to, directing, cinematography, editing, and color-grading. He is currently a junior in BYU's film program and expects to graduate in the spring of 2026.
